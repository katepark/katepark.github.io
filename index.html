Introduction

Welcome to Stargaze.

My name is Francis Hinson. I'm an entrepreneur, developer, and designer. I am 21 years old and currently live in San Francisco.

Deep in the human unconscious is a pervasive need for a logical universe that makes sense. But the real universe is always one step beyond logic.

Frank Herbert, Dune

Stargaze is where I share my thoughts on technology, humanity, and the universe. The content here ranges from software development to international relations to transhumanism. As discontinuous as these themes are, there is a thread I want to tie — I'm curious about artificial intelligence's role in reshaping mankind.

You can contact me at [email protected]. You can also find me on Twitter, LinkedIn, and GitHub. Depending on my availability, I may accept freelance work.

Thanks for visiting.
