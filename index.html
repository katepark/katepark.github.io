About Me

I'm Kate Park. I am a sophomore at Stanford University majoring in computer science. I am the author of Growing Up Aloft, an award-winning orator, and software engineer.

Growing Up Aloft is a sweet and whimsical travel memoir that shows us Europe through the eyes of a child--from tubby toddler to independent teen. During 14 trips, bump into the Prince of Monaco, get snatched by Italian tourists, and fall in love with a Burgundy village. Original and peppy, Growing Up Aloft will charm you with photos, fun facts, and anecdotes.
